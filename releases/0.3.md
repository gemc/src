## Supported platforms

Both x86_64 and ARM64 platforms are supported.

- macOS: 26
- Ubuntu: 24.04
- AlmaLinux: 9.4
- Fedora: 42
- Debian: 13
- Arch Linux: latest

<br/>

## Dependencies

- Geant4: 11.4.1 or higher
- CLHEP: 2.4.6.0 or higher
- Xerces-C: 3.2 or higher
